GRAVE DANGER

ABOUT GRAVE DANGER

How To Play:

  • Create chains of same colored heads, and drop them onto the corresponding island.
  • As you progress, the speed and amount of heads which fall will increase until it reaches lightspeed.
  • The clock is always ticking down, so make matches as fast as you can - a larger chain gains you back more time
  • If you make a chain greater than 2x, the color monster appears and he will eat any type of head.

Info:

GraveDanger is an Open-Source HTML5 action-puzzle game based on chaining color groups together to race against the clock.
While the concept is still somewhat up in the air, we enjoy playing around with various gameplay ideas.
Created by two friends in about one month.

Finalist in Mozilla 2011 Game-On competition
